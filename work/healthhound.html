HealthHound — FigBuild 2026

Designing a wearable system that translates invisible chemical signals into health insights. Inspired by dogs' extraordinary scent detection abilities, we created a product and app that effectively gives humans a new sensory layer for understanding their own health.

Over a weekend hackathon, our team explored how biometric sensing could be made approachable through warm, character-driven product design — bridging hardware constraints with a companion app that surfaces patterns without overwhelming users.

What I'd do differently

I'd push further on onboarding flows for first-time wearable users and run more usability tests on how people interpret chemical-signal data over time, not just at first glance.
