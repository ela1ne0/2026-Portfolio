HackDuke 2026 Visual Identity

The Challenge

Every year, the HackDuke design team selects a unique visual theme for the event. The challenge is to completely overhaul the digital presence to match this theme while improving usability and conversion metrics. I inherited the 2024–25 "Underwater Rave" theme and was tasked with leading the transition to the 2025–26 "Urban Convenience Store" theme.

Strategy & Execution

While I worked within a 5-member team for general site assets, the landing page was my solo project.

  • Thematic Pivot: Transitioned from a dark, ethereal space aesthetic to a warm, lo-fi urban sunset palette with a 2D illustrative style.
  • Solo Execution: Sole designer responsible for the 2026 landing page layout, asset creation, and user flow.
  • Rapid Prototyping: Used Figma Make to wireframe the "Storefront" concept, reducing early iteration time by 40%.
  • Front-End Integration: Worked closely with the HackDuke tech team to implement the site in React with 1:1 fidelity.

Social Media Campaign

Designed countdown and announcement graphics for Instagram with modular Figma templates for daily updates, extending the Convenience Store theme across platforms.

The Impact

  • +80% Increase in Registrations: From ~500 to ~900 participants following the new site launch.
  • Brand Consistency: 20+ digital assets across social channels, boosting online visibility by 50%.

What I'd do differently

I'd establish analytics checkpoints earlier in the redesign process to tie specific UI changes to conversion metrics, and build more reusable motion specs for the dev handoff.
